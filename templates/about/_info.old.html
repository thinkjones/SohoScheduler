What is the S.O.H.O. Scheduler?

The S.O.H.O. Scheduler literally means "Small Office - Home Office Scheduling Application". It is a small business accounting application for individuals in the service scheduling industry. For instance if you run a small business that requires the booking of appointments and the scheduling of service personnel this maybe the application for you.


What Industry Types Would This Be Used For?

The application has been developed in a fairly generic way allowing it to be utilised in a number of different industries. The original intention was for use in a small pre-booking taxi company, however it could be used in a number of industries.
Some examples are:
  • Plumbers
  • Taxi Drivers
  • Tow-Truck Operators
  • Cab Drivers
  • Air Conditioning Booking
